The most essential module

Student Records Management

Centralised student profiles, enrollment history, and academic transcripts — all in one place. No more hunting through folders or chasing administrators for a simple printout.

Administrators can update records in real time, flag anomalies, and generate reports instantly. Audit trails ensure every change is tracked and accountable.

Built with role-based access so faculty, staff, and students each see only what they need. Privacy by design, compliance by default.

About Us

Yogify was founded in 2019 by a team of software engineers who had spent years watching universities struggle with technology that never quite fit. We set out to build the system we wish had existed — one that respects the complexity of academic institutions without adding complexity of its own.

Today, Yogify serves over 80 institutions across 12 countries. Our team of 40 is distributed across Kathmandu, Berlin, and Nairobi. We believe educational technology should be accessible, affordable, and genuinely useful — not a prestige purchase that gathers dust after implementation.
