About
Us

LiftCore was founded on a simple thesis: the best software feels weightless. It lifts teams up instead of slowing them down. We're a studio of 12 engineers — former aerospace, fintech, and open-source contributors — who believe that great infrastructure is invisible.

We work with startups scaling to their first million users and enterprises modernizing legacy systems that are decades old. Our approach is always the same: understand the constraints, strip away the unnecessary, and build something that will still be running cleanly five years from now.
